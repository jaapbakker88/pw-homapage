Why Facebook?

Using Facebook to log in to Party with a Local helps to improve your experience:

  • It makes it quick and easy to sign up and start using Party with a Local straight away.
  • We create your basic Party with a Local profile using some information from your Facebook account (e.g. your name, location, and gender), meaning that you only have to add a bio, photos and your party stats (all optional) in the app to have a complete profile.
  • In terms of safety, it helps to us ensure that the people who join Party with a Local are who they say they are.
