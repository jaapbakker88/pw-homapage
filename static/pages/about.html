What is Party with a Local?

Party with a Local is a free app that connects like-minded travelers and locals that want to party, because a night out anywhere is always better with a local.

Why use Party with a Local?

On your travels ✈️
Anyone who loves to travel knows that the best way to explore a city - and especially the nightlife - is with a local. However, it isn’t always easy to find a like-minded local to show you the best spots in town. That’s why we made Party with a Local - so that when you’re traveling in a new city you can easily find locals that like to meet new people, party and show you around after dark. We wish someone had thought of it sooner!

Between trips 🏠
When you show someone around your own city, it’s a bit like being a traveler yourself. In this way, Party with a Local is a unique way to explore your own city with fresh eyes when you’re between trips. Being the ‘local’ in Party with a Local is a great opportunity to meet like-minded people from around the world, make new friends and create links for your next trip abroad. Everyone wins!

How to use Party with a Local

Last minute meet-ups 🍻
Not one for planning? No problem! Party with a Local is all about spontaneity, allowing locals and travelers to meet up on short notice. Set a Party Status to let people know what you’re up for tonight and see who's partying near you.

Forward planning 💬
Planning a trip abroad and want to get in touch with locals before you arrive? Using the filter function found in the top-right corner of the people screen, you can search to find users in other cities before you arrive.

The aftermath 📝
After you’ve met up with someone, don’t forget to leave a review to help others in the community have a good night out too.🤘🎈🎉
